## About Me

I grew up in the **Bay Area**, which was the perfect place to ask questions like: why does this work this way, who is it for, and how could it be better? That curiosity pushed me toward math, computer science, business, and data. During high school, I earned associate degrees in **Applied Mathematics** and **Computer Science** from community college, then continued into Data Science at UCSD.

My background is intentionally cross-functional:

- **Data Science:** modeling, experimentation, statistical thinking, feature engineering, data visualization, and end-to-end analysis.
- **Product:** product strategy, PRDs, user research, MVP scoping, competitive analysis, and translating insights into decisions.
- **AI:** LLM workflows, RAG-style research systems, prompt evaluation, AI-assisted synthesis, and applied AI product thinking.
- **Finance and Business:** financial modeling, budget variance analysis, revenue and cash flow analysis, GAAP fundamentals, bookkeeping, and QuickBooks.
- **Consulting and Research:** market sizing, competitive intelligence, client deliverables, healthcare tech research, and executive-ready narratives.

Right now, my interest is centered on **product management for AI and data products**: tools that help people make better decisions, move faster, and understand complex systems without hiding the nuance.

## Featured Projects

### Project Insight: AI Market Research Platform

[![Project Insight](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Project--Insight-181717?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/sonalishyma/Project-Insight)

An AI-powered market research platform that generates structured company analyses, competitor insights, and strategic summaries using **FastAPI**, **OpenRouter**, and large language models.

What I focused on:

- turning fragmented research sources into structured strategic summaries
- designing an API-first architecture for future dashboard and real-time search integration
- using LLMs to reduce research cycle time while preserving analytical rigor
- creating outputs that are useful for product managers, analysts, and strategy teams

### AI Market Research

Built an AI-powered research pipeline that ingests analyst reports, earnings calls, and news to surface competitive signals faster than manual research.

Key ideas:

- RAG-style retrieval over static fine-tuning because market context changes quickly
- structured output schemas for consistent downstream UI rendering
- confidence scoring to surface uncertainty instead of hiding it
- analyst-centered synthesis rather than fully automated decision-making

### Football Analytics

A data science deep dive into NFL performance metrics, focused on finding which signals actually predict winning beyond traditional box score stats.

Highlights:

- analyzed multi-season play-by-play data
- compared logistic regression, random forest, and gradient boosting
- prioritized interpretability because sports analytics depends on stakeholder trust
- explored context-adjusted metrics like EPA, third-down conversion, and red zone performance

### Psychology Clinic Website

Designed and built a warm, credible web presence for a psychologist launching a private practice.

Product and design focus:

- balanced warmth with professional trust
- designed a conversion flow for first-time visitors
- used founder story and credibility signals to improve inquiry intent
- approached the website as a service product, not just a static page

### Product Strategy Case Studies

I write strategy briefs that reverse-engineer why companies build the way they do.

Recent examples from my portfolio:

- **Meta x Kylie Jenner:** a case study on AI glasses, celebrity distribution, platform control, and trust risk.
- **AI Algorithms in Music Apps:** a strategy breakdown of Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube Music through the lens of recommendation systems and business models.

## How I Work

**Data-first decisions.** I root product and strategy decisions in analysis, not vibes. That means building models, testing assumptions, and turning numbers into a clear recommendation.

**Ship fast, learn faster.** I like lightweight prototypes, fast validation, and learning from real signal early.

**Clarity over complexity.** Dense work only matters if people can use it. I care about making analysis legible through strong writing, visuals, product specs, and stakeholder narratives.

**AI with judgment.** I do not think AI replaces analyst or product judgment. I think it amplifies strong judgment when the system is grounded, transparent, and honest about uncertainty.
